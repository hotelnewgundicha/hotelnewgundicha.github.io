Welcome to Hotel New Gundicha

Hotel New Gundicha in Rayagada is renowned for its delectable fusion of Indian, Chinese, and Tandoori cuisines. This restaurant offers a diverse menu that caters to both vegetarian and non-vegetarian preferences, making it a popular dining destination for locals and visitors alike.

Ambiance

The restaurant boasts a warm and inviting ambiance, with comfortable seating arrangements suitable for both family gatherings and intimate dinners. The decor reflects a blend of traditional and contemporary styles, enhancing the dining experience with a cozy and pleasant atmosphere.

Browse Menu

Indian Cuisine: The Indian menu features a variety of classic dishes, from rich and creamy curries to spicy and flavorful biryanis. Signature dishes include Butter Chicken, Paneer Butter Masala, and an assortment of traditional Indian breads like Naan and Roti.

Chinese Cuisine: The Chinese offerings range from savory stir-fries to flavorful noodles and soups. Popular dishes include Schezwan Noodles, Manchurian, and Sweet and Sour Chicken, all prepared with authentic ingredients and techniques.
